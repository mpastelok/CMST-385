Pink Floyd at Pompeii - MCMLXXII

January 19, 2026

Pink Floyd's legendary 1972 concert film Pink Floyd at Pompeii - MCMLXXII has been fully restored and released on 4K Ultra HD Blu-Ray with upgraded audio in Dolby Atmos, 5.1, and stereo formats. The new edition captures the band performing in the ancient Roman amphitheater without an audience, featuring classic live renditions of tracks like “Echoes” and “One of These Days.” The restoration brings enhanced visuals and immersive sound, giving fans an unprecedented way to experience this iconic performance.

Pink Floyd's Wish You Were Here 50 Out Now

December 12, 2025

Pink Floyd has released Wish You Were Here 50, celebrating the 50th anniversary of the band's classic 1975 album Wish You Were Here. This special edition features multiple discs of studio rarities, alternate mixes, and previously unreleased material, giving fans a fresh look at one of the band's most beloved records. Critics have praised the release, noting both the emotional depth of the original work and the excitement around the new material.
