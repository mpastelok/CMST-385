About Me

I'm Matt, a digital artist focused on visual storytelling, atmospheric worldbuilding, and immersive imagery.

My work spans digital compositing, 3D modeling, motion graphics, and illustration, with a strong interest in how lighting, color, and environmental detail shape mood and narrative.

Academic Journey

I hold an Associate's Degree in Business Administration (2016) and am currently completing my Bachelor's in Web and Digital Design at UMGC. Along the way, I've earned a Digital Design Certificate and am pursuing an Augmented/VR Design Certificate, with graduation planned for Summer 2026.

Creative Philosophy

I am drawn to projects that allow me to build emotionally driven worlds and concepts, whether that takes the form of a cinematic still image, a symbolic fantasy piece, or a narrative-based motion project. Lately, much of my strongest work has been rooted in Photoshop compositing, where I enjoy refining imagery through mood, symbolism, and atmosphere. While digital design is my core focus, I actively utilize 3D modeling and web technologies to ensure my visual storytelling is both immersive and functionally impactful across different digital mediums.
