Cosmo

This stylized portrait features my cat, Cosmo, reimagined as an astronaut drifting through space.

Precise compositing, perspective alignment, and detailed lighting work were used to seamlessly integrate the character into the suit and helmet. A vibrant nebula backdrop and polished finishing touches create a playful yet cinematic final image depicting his 'lost in space' attitude.
